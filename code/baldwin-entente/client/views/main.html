Entente

Connecting like-minded people on important issues.

The word ‘entente’ (from the French entente) means ‘an alliance of parties to such an understanding.’ The goal of this application is to connect like-minded users on important social and environmental issues by geographical region.

To start, feel free to explore Entente by geographical region or environmental/social concern. Then check out those in your area and elsewhere in the world who share your concerns and interests, and connect via our chat feature.
